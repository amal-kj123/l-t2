Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 35 minutes.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

  • 300 g pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 1 cup broccoli, zucchini, peppers, carrots, asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté veggies with garlic and olive oil.
  3. Toss pasta with veggies and seasonings.
  4. Serve hot with Parmesan and herbs.

Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500 g fresh fish
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Spices: turmeric, chili powder, coriander
  • Tomato, curry leaves, garlic, ginger
  • Kudampuli (gambooge)

Instructions

  1. Prepare spice mix with coconut oil.
  2. Add tomatoes, curry leaves, and kudampuli.
  3. Simmer with water, then add fish pieces.
  4. Cook 10 minutes and rest before serving.
