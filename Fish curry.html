Kottayam Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500 g fresh fish pieces
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1–2 sprigs curry leaves
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1–2 tbsp kudampuli (gambooge), soaked
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak kudampuli in warm water for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat coconut oil, add mustard & fenugreek seeds.
  3. Sauté curry leaves, green chilies, onion, ginger & garlic.
  4. Add chili powder, coriander powder & turmeric.
  5. Mix in tomatoes & soaked kudampuli water.
  6. Add fish pieces, cook 8–10 minutes.
  7. Finish with coconut oil drizzle & curry leaves.
